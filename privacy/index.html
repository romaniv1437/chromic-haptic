🔇 Chromic Haptic

Privacy Policy — Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Our Promise

Chromic Haptic is an accessibility tool. We built it to help Deaf and hard-of-hearing people feel music through vibrations. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Period.

2. What Data We Access — and Why

2.1 SoundCloud Links You Provide

When you paste a SoundCloud URL, we send that URL to our processing servers (hosted on Modal). The server downloads the audio temporarily, analyzes it with AI to extract instruments and lyrics, and generates a vibration pattern. The audio file is deleted immediately after processing. We never store your audio on disk longer than necessary to process it.

2.2 Locally Stored Data

Everything stays on your device:

  • Cache: Processed vibration patterns and downloaded audio are saved locally so you don't re-download the same track twice. Stored in the app's private documents directory.
  • History: Your last 50 tracks (title, URL) are stored locally in a JSON file. This is purely for your convenience.
  • Preferences: Your chosen haptic mode (beat, instrumental, word, both, full) is saved locally.

None of this data leaves your phone.

3. Subscriptions (Chromic Pro)

Chromic offers an optional Pro subscription ($4.99/month) that unlocks unlimited tracks and all vibration modes. Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat solely to verify your subscription status.

RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase token from Apple/Google — not your name, email, or payment info. This token tells us "this device has an active Pro subscription" without identifying you personally. RevenueCat's privacy policy.

4. No Analytics, No Trackers

Chromic Haptic v1.0 contains zero analytics, zero ad SDKs, zero tracking pixels. We have no idea how you use the app, which tracks you play, or how often you open it. We might add anonymous crash reporting in the future (to fix bugs faster), but we'll update this policy and ask for consent first.

5. Children

Chromic is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. If you believe we inadvertently received data from a child, contact us and we'll delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions or concerns, email us — we'll respond within 48 hours.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (for example, if we add optional crash reporting), we'll notify you in the app and update the "Last updated" date above. The current version is always available at this URL.
